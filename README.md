# A-Global-Phenomenon-Taylor-Swift-s-Eras-Tour
This project explores the global impact of Taylor Swift's Eras Tour through data visualizations styled for the Economist. It highlights the tour's record-setting music metrics and its spillover effects on the hospitality industry and local economies. 

## 🎯 Objective
Communicate the scale and reach of the Eras Tour had both musically and economically through concise, print-ready charts and maps. The project emphasizes thoughtful chart selection, clean typography, and a cohesive visual narrative.

## 📊 Tools Used
- Adobe Illustrator (illustrations and design)
- Flourish (chart creation)
- Excel and Numbers (data preparation and cleaning)

## 📚 Data Sources
- Music & business reporting: Rolling Stone, Billboard, Variety, Business Insider, TIME, Boardroom  
- Box office: Box Office Mojo  
- Hospitality/Travel: STR and Lighthouse (formerly OTA Insight), CNN Travel  
- Official Taylor Swift site for contextual details

## 🗺️ Visual Elements
- **World map** of tour stops and regions covered  
- **Lollipop chart**: top 10 highest-grossing tours of all time  
- **Column chart**: *Eras Tour* concert film box office (domestic / international / worldwide box office)  
- **Line chart**: Taylor Swift tour revenues over time  
- **Waterfall chart**: hotel price changes before/during/after North American dates  
- **Area chart**: hotel unavailability in Stockholm around show dates  
- **Dual line chart**: hotel occupancy in Melbourne vs. Sydney during the tour window

> Chart types were selected for label legibility, correct visual encodings (length/area), and alignment with *The Economist* style.
